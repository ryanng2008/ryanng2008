Signs and symptoms of phobias

  • Physical symptoms
  • sweating.
  • trembling.
  • hot flashes or chills.
  • shortness of breath or difficulty breathing.
  • a choking sensation.
  • rapid heartbeat (tachycardia)
  • pain or tightness in the chest.
  • a sensation of butterflies in the stomach.

The effect of phobia-related disorders

  • Intense fear; anxiety in response to a specific trigger (such as a certain animal, situation, or object) - disproportionate to the actual threat posed by the trigger
  • Avoidance of the feared trigger, which can disrupt daily activities and interfere with work, school, and relationships
  • Physical symptoms of anxiety - eg rapid heartbeat, sweating, shaking, and nausea
  • Shame or embarrassment about the phobia - can lead to social isolation and decreased self-esteem
  • Negative impact on mental health, potentially leading to depression and anxiety disorders Interference with daily activities and quality of life
  • Difficulty forming or maintaining relationships due to fear and avoidance behaviors
  • Increased risk of substance abuse or other forms of self-medication to manage the symptoms of phobia-related disorders
  • Interference with work or academic performance
  • In severe cases - Can lead to panic attacks, which are intense episodes of fear and anxiety that can last for several minutes.

Types of 'phobia'-related disorders

Disorder Definition
Agoraphobia Fear of being in situations where escape might be difficult or embarrassing.
Social Phobia (Social Anxiety Disorder) Fear of being humiliated, embarrassed, or judged by others.
Specific Phobia Intense fear of a specific object or situation.
Acrophobia Fear of heights.
Arachnophobia Fear of spiders.
Claustrophobia Fear of confined spaces.
Aerophobia Fear of flying.
Trypanophobia Fear of injections or medical procedures involving needles.
Hydrophobia Fear of water.
Dentophobia Fear of dentists or dental procedures.
Ophidiophobia Fear of snakes.
Mysophobia Fear of germs or contamination.
Zoophobia Fear of animals.
Entomophobia Fear of insects.
Astraphobia Fear of thunder and lightning.
Nyctophobia Fear of the dark.
Ornithophobia Fear of birds.
